About Us

From cold-pressed juices to grilled street corn and citrus-marinated proteins, everything is made fresh daily. We bring the sunbaked charm of the Mexican desert into a modern, elevated dining space.


In the heart of the desert, there's a plant that thrives under the sun, stores water in its body, and nourishes those who know how to use it. That plant is the nopal cactus; it is the soul of our story.


Nopalito was born from a deep appreciation for the natural simplicity and bold flavors found across Mexico's desert regions, from Oaxaca to Sonora. It is a love letter to fresh ingredients, vibrant culture, and the enduring spirit of resilience and hospitality that defines Mexican food.
